Why I Love Chess

Chess is a game of strategy and discipline. It challenges you to think ahead and adapt to different situations. I enjoy playing chess casually with friends and more competitively online.

About Chess

Chess is a two-player board game played on an 8x8 grid. Each player controls an army of 16 pieces, including a king, queen, 2 rooks, 2 bishops, 2 knights, and 8 pawns. In a nutshell, the goal is to checkmate the opponents king while protecting your own.

Fun Fact

Did you know? The number of possible chess games exceeds the number of atoms in the observable universe!
